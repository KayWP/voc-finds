VOC Finds

The archives of the Dutch East India Company hold a vast and complex history. In the Netherlands alone, they comprise more than eight million pages of material. Within these records lie countless remarkable discoveries, from maps and images to unique documents, that offer glimpses into the past. This gallery highlights a selection of intriguing items from the collection. Who knows? One of them might spark inspiration for your next research journey.
